Quality System

Our Quality Commitment

Quality is not just a certificate on the wall — it's embedded in every step of our manufacturing process.

🔬

Material Inspection

All raw materials undergo spectrometer analysis and are verified against mill certificates. We maintain full material traceability from source to finished product.

📐

Dimensional Control

CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) inspection ensures every dimension meets specification. Tolerances maintained to ±0.01mm on critical features.

💧

Pressure Testing

Hydrostatic and pneumatic pressure testing validates the integrity of every fitting. Test pressures exceed rated working pressures by standard safety factors.

🔍

Surface Inspection

Visual and dimensional inspection of surface finish, thread quality, and coating integrity. Defective parts are rejected before reaching packaging.

📊

Statistical Process Control

SPC monitoring on critical dimensions ensures consistent quality across production batches. Process capability indices (Cpk) tracked continuously.

📋

Documentation

Complete inspection reports, material certificates, and test reports available upon request. Full traceability maintained for every order.

📜

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our quality management system is certified to ISO 9001:2015, the internationally recognized standard for quality management.

This certification covers:

  • Design and development of hydraulic components
  • CNC machining and welding processes
  • Quality control and inspection procedures
  • Customer service and after-sales support

Certificate available for download upon request.
