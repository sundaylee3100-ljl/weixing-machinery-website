Who We Are

Founded in Yuyao, Zhejiang — known as the "Valve and Pump Capital of China" — Yuyao Wei Xing Machinery Co., Ltd has been a trusted manufacturer of precision hydraulic components for over 20 years.

We specialize in hydraulic fittings, welding assemblies, and valve components, serving industries including construction, oil & gas, agriculture, marine, mining, and manufacturing across 30+ countries worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced CNC machining centers, enabling us to maintain tolerances of ±0.01mm while producing both standard catalog parts and custom-engineered solutions.
